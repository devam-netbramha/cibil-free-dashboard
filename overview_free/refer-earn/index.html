Frequently Asked Questions

How do I qualify to be a referrer?

You should be an existing consumer to be able to refer your friends and family.

Visit the “Refer & Earn” tab on your dashboard page to view the unique referral code on the free/paid dashboard on web/mobile/app versions.

How many reward points do I get on each referral?

Each referral entitles you to receive 15% of the subscription amount as reward points, subject to the referee successfully subscribing to a paid plan.

How can I check the status of my referrals?

Sign into your CIBIL account. Open your ‘Rewards Program page. Open the ‘Your Reward Wallet’ section. Click on ‘Redeem Points’ button and check the status of your referrals.’

When will I get my reward?

Once the referee successfully completes their subscription, the reward points will be credited in your reward wallet within 48 hours.

What can I purchase with my reward points?

The points you accumulate (maximum 1500 points) can be used to renew your own subscription.

How can I share my referral code?

You will be able to copy and share the referral link or code to your intended referee via SMS, Email, WhatsApp, Instagram, Twitter or Facebook.
